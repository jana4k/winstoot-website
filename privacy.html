Institute, Admin, Teachers/Professor, Students

a) For School or College Admin Create your Institute or Organisation Profile. Once that is done you would receive a unique code. Share that code with your institute Teachers or Professors & add them to the institute with individual verification.

b) For Teachers & Professors i. Admin -> Teachers & Professors If you have received the Institute code from Admin, Go to the classroom tab and use the code to get into the digital classroom.

ii. Teachers & Professors -> Student After getting into the digital classroom, Create your class. You would receive a Unique Class Code. Share that code with your student and invite them into your class with individual verification.

c) For Students If you have received the Class Code from your Teachers or Professors, Go to the classroom tab and use the code to get in the digital classroom.

Winstoot is very powerful & advanced teaching & learning platform. Admin can manage their institute through the application. Teachers / Professors can enroll students in their institute’s digital classroom with authorized class code, they can create their classroom, add their subjects, conduct online live classes, upload video lessons, online tests, Instant notification thorough noticeboard option, assignment, quiz, upload reference study materials, online attendance, download attendance report based on date & period.

they can create classroom chat groups in order to share education-related materials through that or to have an education-oriented discussion.

Students can easily connect with their classroom with the authorized class code. Easy to understand UI is very helpful for the students. Everyday activities are reflected in the app. Video lessons are uploaded by teachers in the app. So whenever needed students can refer to the video lessons which will be very easy to understand.
